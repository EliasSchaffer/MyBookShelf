\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
My\+Book\+Shelf is an open-\/source Java application designed to help users organize and track their personal book collection. Whether you\textquotesingle{}re an avid reader or a casual book lover, My\+Book\+Shelf allows you to manage your reading list, track books you\textquotesingle{}ve read, and plan future reads all in one place.\hypertarget{README.md_autotoc_md1}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Features}{Features}}\label{README.md_autotoc_md1}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\bfseries{Book Collection Management}}\+: Add, edit, and remove books from your collection.
\item {\bfseries{Customizable Categories}}\+: Organize your books by genres, authors, or custom tags.
\item {\bfseries{Bookshelf Overview}}\+: View all your books in an organized and easy-\/to-\/use interface.
\item {\bfseries{Search Functionality}}\+: Quickly search for books by title, author, or category.
\item {\bfseries{Reading Goals}}\+: Set and track your reading goals throughout the year.
